<p><p>The air cargo insulated containers market comprises two primary types: single bubble and double bubble containers. Single bubble containers feature a singular layer of insulation, providing basic temperature control for sensitive goods. In contrast, double bubble containers consist of two layers of insulation, offering enhanced thermal protection and stability for temperature-sensitive shipments. Both types are essential for transporting perishables, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive items, ensuring product integrity during transit while optimizing cost and efficiency in air freight services.</p></p>
