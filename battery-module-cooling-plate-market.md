<p><strong>The Battery Module Cooling Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Cooling</li><li>Direct Cold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Module Cooling Plate market features two primary types: liquid cooling and direct cold cooling. Liquid cooling utilizes a fluid medium to efficiently transfer heat away from battery modules, ensuring optimal temperature regulation during operation. In contrast, direct cold cooling involves the placement of cooling systems in direct contact with battery surfaces, offering immediate heat removal and enhanced thermal management. Both methods are crucial in improving battery performance, lifespan, and safety in electric vehicles and energy storage systems.</p></p>
