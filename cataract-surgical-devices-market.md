<p><p>The cataract surgical devices market encompasses several key types essential for cataract surgery. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are implanted to restore vision after cataract removal. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) help maintain eye shape and protect tissues during surgery. Phacoemulsification devices use ultrasound to break up cataracts for easier removal. Cataract surgery lasers offer precise, computer-guided techniques for lens removal. IOL injectors facilitate the implantation of lenses into the eye efficiently and safely, enhancing surgical outcomes.</p></p>
