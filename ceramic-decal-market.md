<p><p>The ceramic decal market is primarily categorized into two types: digital decals and silkscreen decals. Digital decals involve using digital printing techniques to create designs directly onto transfer paper, allowing for intricate and customizable patterns. In contrast, silkscreen decals rely on traditional screen-printing methods, where inks are pressed through a mesh screen, providing durability and vibrant colors. Both types cater to various applications in ceramics, enhancing aesthetic appeal and personalization in products like dinnerware, tiles, and decorative items.</p></p>
