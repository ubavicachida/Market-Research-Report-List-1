<p><p>The circulating water coolers market comprises several types of chillers, each serving distinct applications. Reciprocating chillers use pistons to compress refrigerant, making them efficient for small to medium-sized systems. Centrifugal chillers employ a rotating shaft to achieve high cooling capacities, ideal for large-scale operations. Screw chillers utilize twin screws for compression, offering energy efficiency and reliability. Additionally, the "Other" market segment includes alternative cooling solutions like absorption chillers and thermal energy storage systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
