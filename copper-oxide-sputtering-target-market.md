<p><p>The Copper Oxide Sputtering Target market comprises two primary types: Rotatable Transformation and Non-Rotating Type. Rotatable Transformation targets allow for continuous rotation during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency by maximizing the utilization of the target material. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets remain stationary, typically used for smaller applications or where rotational systems are impractical. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing, thin-film deposition, and other fields, influencing operational efficiency and cost-effectiveness in production processes.</p></p>
