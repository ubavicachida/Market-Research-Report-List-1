<p><p>Decentralized Clinical Trials (DCTs) are innovative clinical study designs that leverage digital technologies to conduct research remotely, allowing participants to engage from home or other convenient locations. This approach enhances patient accessibility, recruitment, and retention by minimizing travel burdens and improving overall participant experience. As a response to evolving patient needs and technological advancements, DCTs enable real-time data collection through wearable devices, mobile applications, and telehealth consultations, facilitating more robust and timely insights.</p><p>The DCTs market is projected to experience substantial growth, with an expected CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period. Factors driving this expansion include an increasing focus on patient-centric approaches, advances in digital health technologies, and the growing demand for faster, more cost-effective study methodologies. The COVID-19 pandemic further accelerated the adoption of DCTs by highlighting the need for remote monitoring and data collection. </p><p>Recent trends within the market show a rising integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning in trial management, as well as partnerships between biopharmaceutical companies and technology providers to enhance operational efficiency.
