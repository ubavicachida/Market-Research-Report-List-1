<p><p>The electrochemical sensor market comprises three main types: potentiometric, amperometric, and conductometric sensors. Potentiometric sensors measure the voltage change in response to ion concentration, commonly used in pH measurement. Amperometric sensors detect current generated from electrochemical reactions, ideal for monitoring gases and glucose levels. Conductometric sensors assess the conductivity of solutions, useful for determining ion concentrations in water quality analysis. Each type serves unique applications, driving advancements in environmental monitoring, medical diagnostics, and industrial processes.</p></p>
