<p><ul><li>All-flash Array</li><li>Hybrid Flash Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flash-based array market consists of two main types: all-flash arrays and hybrid flash arrays. All-flash arrays use only NAND flash storage, offering high performance, low latency, and energy efficiency for data-intensive applications. In contrast, hybrid flash arrays combine flash storage with traditional hard disk drives (HDDs), providing a balanced approach for cost and performance. This enables organizations to optimize storage resources while benefiting from faster data access. Both types cater to varying enterprise storage needs based on performance and budget.</p></p>
