<p><p>The flexible swing doors market is divided into two main types: push version and pull version. The push version is designed for users to open the door by applying pressure on it, making it ideal for high-traffic areas where hands may be occupied. Both versions enhance accessibility and functionality in various environments, including hospitals, restaurants, and warehouses.</p></p>
