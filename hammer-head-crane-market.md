<p><ul><li>Top Slewing</li><li>Bottom Slewing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hammerhead crane market is primarily segmented into top slewing and bottom slewing types. Top slewing cranes feature a horizontal jib that is mounted on a tall vertical mast, offering high lifting capacity and suitable for large construction projects. In contrast, bottom slewing cranes have a rotating superstructure situated at ground level, providing better mobility and flexibility for smaller site operations. These distinctions influence their application, with each type catering to specific project requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
