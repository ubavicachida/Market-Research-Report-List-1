<p><strong>The Headlight Bulb Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>Halogen</li><li>Xenon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The headlight bulb market features three main types: LED, Halogen, and Xenon. LED bulbs are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and offer bright illumination, making them increasingly popular. Halogen bulbs provide a warm light and are commonly found in standard vehicles; they are affordable but have a shorter lifespan. Xenon, or HID (High-Intensity Discharge) bulbs, emit a strong, bluish-white light, enhancing visibility but often come at a higher cost and complexity in installation. Each type serves different preferences and vehicle needs.</p></p>
