<p><p>The IC forklift market is categorized into three main types: gasoline, diesel, and liquid propane. Gasoline forklifts are typically lighter and suited for indoor use, offering lower emissions and quieter operation. Diesel forklifts are designed for heavy-duty applications, excelling in outdoor environments with stronger power and efficiency, but they produce higher emissions. Liquid propane forklifts strike a balance, providing versatility for both indoor and outdoor use, with lower emissions than gasoline and diesel options while maintaining robust performance.</p></p>
