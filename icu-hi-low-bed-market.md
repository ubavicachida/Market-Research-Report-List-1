<p><p>The ICU hi-low bed market includes three main types: electric beds, semi-electric beds, and manual beds. Electric beds offer powered adjustments for height and position, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver access. Semi-electric beds combine powered head and foot adjustments with manual height control, providing a balance of convenience and cost-effectiveness. Manual beds require full manual operation for adjustments, making them the most economical option but less convenient for staff. Each type serves specific patient needs and care environments.</p></p>
