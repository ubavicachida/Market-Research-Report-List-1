<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental</li><li>Absolute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive encoders are essential in motion control systems and are classified into two main market types: incremental and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders provide relative position data based on changes in movement, generating pulses as the shaft rotates, making them suitable for applications where speed and direction are critical. Absolute encoders, on the other hand, deliver a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring precise tracking even after power loss. Both types cater to different industrial needs based on accuracy and application requirements.</p></p>
