<p><ul><li>Self-adhesive or Pressure Sensitive Labellers</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Labellers</li><li>Glue Based Labellers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The labeling and coding machinery market includes several types: self-adhesive or pressure-sensitive labellers, shrink sleeve labellers, and glue-based labellers. Self-adhesive labellers apply labels using adhesive backing, allowing for quick application on various surfaces. Shrink sleeve labellers use heat to shrink a plastic sleeve around products, providing a seamless label. Glue-based labellers utilize adhesive to affix labels onto containers, ensuring durability. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific packaging needs across diverse industries, enhancing branding and compliance.</p></p>
