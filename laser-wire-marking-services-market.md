<p><p>Laser wire marking services are categorized into two main types: permanent marking and non-permanent marking. Permanent marking involves the use of lasers to create lasting identifiers on wires or cables, ensuring that markings endure harsh conditions and do not fade over time. This type is ideal for applications requiring durability, such as in aerospace and automotive industries. Conversely, non-permanent marking allows for temporary labels or identifiers that can be easily altered or removed, suited for applications with frequent updates or changes.</p></p>
