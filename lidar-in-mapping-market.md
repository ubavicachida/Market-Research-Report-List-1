The market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed components for rapid scanning and is more compact and durable, making it ideal for autonomous vehicles. Mechanical LiDAR, on the other hand, employs rotating components to capture 360-degree data, offering high accuracy but often at the expense of mobility and size.</p></p>
