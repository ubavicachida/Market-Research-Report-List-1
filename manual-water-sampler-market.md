<p><strong>The Manual Water Sampler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Discrete Water Sampler"</li><li>"Composite Water Sampler"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual water sampler market comprises two main types: discrete water samplers and composite water samplers. Discrete water samplers collect individual samples at specific time intervals or locations, providing precise data for specific conditions or events. In contrast, composite water samplers aggregate samples over a defined period, offering a representative analysis of water quality over that time. Both types are essential for monitoring environmental conditions, pollution levels, and compliance with regulatory standards in various water bodies.</p></p>
