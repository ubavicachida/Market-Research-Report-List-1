<p><p>The marine annunciators market encompasses three primary types of signaling systems: sound signals, pressure signals, and optical signals. Sound signals utilize audible alarms to alert crew members to critical conditions, ensuring immediate attention. Pressure signals involve monitoring changes in pressure levels, often indicating equipment malfunctions or system failures. Optical signals utilize visual indicators such as lights or displays to convey warnings. Together, these systems enhance safety and operational efficiency in maritime settings by providing clear and prompt alerts to potential hazards.</p></p>
