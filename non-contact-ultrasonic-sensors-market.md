<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Sealed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic sensors are categorized into open type and sealed type based on their design and application. Open type sensors allow direct exposure to the environment, making them suitable for applications where airflow or gas detection is crucial. In contrast, sealed type sensors are encased to protect internal components from harsh conditions, ensuring reliability in challenging environments. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, from automation and robotics to automotive and manufacturing, enhancing measurement accuracy without requiring physical contact.</p></p>
