<p><p>Non-destructive coating thickness gauges are classified based on the technology used for measurement. Magnetic and Eddy Current techniques rely on magnetic properties and electrical conductivity, respectively, making them suitable for ferrous and non-ferrous substrates. Ultrasonic technology uses sound waves to assess thickness, ideal for coatings on various materials. X-ray technology employs radiation for precise measurements, particularly in complex coatings. Other methods may include laser or optical techniques, offering alternatives for specific applications and industries. These technologies enable reliable assessments without damaging the substrate.</p></p>
