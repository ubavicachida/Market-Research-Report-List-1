<p><p>The Non-Protein Nitrogen (NPN) market primarily encompasses Urea, Ammonia, and Biuret, each serving vital roles in agriculture and animal feed. Urea is widely used as a nitrogen source for crops, promoting growth and yield. Ammonia, both anhydrous and aqueous, enhances soil fertility and is crucial in producing fertilizers. Biuret, a byproduct of urea synthesis, acts as a slow-release nitrogen source, optimizing nutrient uptake in plants. Together, these NPN types support efficient agricultural practices and sustainable crop production.</p></p>
