<p><strong>The Opacimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Paper Opacimeter</li><li>Transmissive Opacimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Opacimeter market is divided into two primary types: Filter Paper Opacimeter and Transmissive Opacimeter. Filter Paper Opacimeters measure light scattering and opacity in filter paper materials, making them essential for quality control in the paper industry. In contrast, Transmissive Opacimeters assess the opacity of transparent or semi-transparent materials by measuring the light transmission through the sample. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as packaging, textiles, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring products meet specific opacity standards.</p></p>
