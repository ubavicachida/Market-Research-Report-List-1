<p><p>The packaging machinery market encompasses various segments, each specializing in different functionalities. Form-fill-seal (FFS) machinery automates packing by forming containers, filling them, and sealing them in one process. Labeling machinery applies labels to products for identification and branding. Coding machinery ensures products are marked with essential information like expiration dates. Sealing machinery focuses on securely closing packages, while wrapping and bundling machinery groups products together for efficient handling. Other machinery includes items like conveyors and strapping machines that support the overall packaging process.</p></p>
