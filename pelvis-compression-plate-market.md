<p><strong>The Pelvis Compression Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Locking</li><li>Non-Locking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pelvis Compression Plate market is segmented into two main types: locking and non-locking plates. Locking plates feature screws that engage with the plate, providing enhanced stability and fixation, which is crucial for complex fractures. Non-locking plates, on the other hand, rely on compression between the plate and the bone for stability, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types serve distinct clinical needs, impacting their application in orthopedic surgeries for pelvis injuries.</p></p>
