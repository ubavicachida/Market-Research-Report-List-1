<p><p>The Polarization Maintaining (PM) Fiber Optic Attenuator market comprises two main types: Variable Optical Attenuators (VOA) and PM Variable Optical Attenuators (PMVOA). VOAs are designed to control signal strength by adjusting light intensity in a fiber optic system, without preserving polarization. In contrast, PMVOAs maintain the polarization of the light signal while attenuating it, making them essential for applications sensitive to polarization changes, such as in coherent communication systems. Both types help optimize signal quality in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
