<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential ventilation fans are crucial for maintaining indoor air quality and comfort. The market primarily includes two types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans generate airflow by spinning air radially, making them suitable for high-pressure systems, ideal for larger spaces. In contrast, axial fans move air along the axis of the fan blade, producing higher airflow with lower pressure, making them effective for removing stale air in smaller rooms. Both types cater to diverse ventilation needs in homes.</p></p>
