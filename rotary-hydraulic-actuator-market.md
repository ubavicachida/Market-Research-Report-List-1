<p><strong>The Rotary Hydraulic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion</li><li>Vane Type</li><li>Combination type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary hydraulic actuator market can be categorized into three main types: rack and pinion, vane type, and combination type. Rack and pinion actuators utilize a gear mechanism to convert hydraulic pressure into rotary motion, making them ideal for precise control. Vane type actuators employ rotating vanes within a housing to achieve torque, offering smooth operation and compact design. Combination type actuators integrate features of both rack and pinion and vane types, providing enhanced versatility and performance for various applications.</p></p>
