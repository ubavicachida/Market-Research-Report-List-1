<p><p>Sales management tools are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, easier updates, and remote access. This model is cost-effective for many businesses, allowing for flexible subscription plans. On-premises tools, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's servers. They provide greater control and customization but require more significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
