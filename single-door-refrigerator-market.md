Freezing models are designed to maintain extremely low temperatures for longer-term food storage, ensuring items remain frozen. Refrigerator-freezer combinations offer both functionalities, allowing consumers to refrigerate fresh foods and freeze items in a single unit, maximizing space and convenience in compact living environments.</p></p>
