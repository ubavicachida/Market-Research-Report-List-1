<p><p>The smart baby monitor market comprises three main types: internet baby monitors, video baby monitors, and audio baby monitors. Internet baby monitors utilize Wi-Fi connectivity to stream real-time video and audio to smartphones or tablets, offering remote access. Video baby monitors provide visual monitoring with camera features, allowing parents to see their baby while also hearing sounds. Audio baby monitors focus solely on sound transmission, enabling parents to listen for their baby's cues without visual feedback. Each type serves unique parental needs for child safety and comfort.</p></p>
