<p><p>The two-wheeler keyless entry system market features mainly two types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows riders to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing a button, relying on proximity sensors to detect the key fob's presence. In contrast, RKES requires users to press a button on the remote control to unlock or start the bike from a distance. Both systems enhance convenience and security, catering to the growing demand for advanced motorcycle features.</p></p>
