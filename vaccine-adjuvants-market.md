<p><p>The vaccine adjuvants market is primarily categorized into three types: aluminum adjuvants, emulsions, and others. Aluminum adjuvants are the most widely used, enhancing immune response and stability in vaccines. Emulsions, which can include oil-in-water or water-in-oil mixtures, improve the delivery of antigens, facilitating a stronger immune reaction. Other adjuvant types encompass a variety of innovative substances, including saponins and polymer-based adjuvants, designed to optimize vaccine efficacy and broaden the response against pathogens.</p></p>
