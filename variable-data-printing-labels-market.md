<p><p>Variable Data Printing (VDP) labels enable customized printing for various applications, with two main market types: release liner and linerless labels. Release liner labels consist of a backing material that peels away to expose an adhesive surface, allowing easy application. They are commonly used for shipping and product labeling. Linerless labels eliminate the backing, reducing waste and enhancing efficiency. These labels allow for continuous printing, making them ideal for high-volume applications in industries like food and beverage, logistics, and retail.</p></p>
