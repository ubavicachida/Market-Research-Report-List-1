<p><strong>The Vehicle Emission Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle emission analyzers market includes several key types. Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers measure gas concentrations using infrared light, effective for detecting CO and CO2. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) analyze hydrocarbons by ionizing them in a flame, providing high sensitivity. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) detect nitrogen oxides through light emission during chemical reactions. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect exhaust samples for comprehensive analysis of pollutants. Other types include advanced technologies like optical and laser-based systems, catering to diverse emission testing needs.</p></p>
