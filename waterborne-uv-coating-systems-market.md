<p><p>Waterborne UV coating systems encompass two primary market types: hardware devices and chemical reagents. Hardware devices include the application equipment, such as UV curing lamps and spray systems, designed to efficiently apply and cure coatings. Chemical reagents refer to the actual waterborne UV coating formulations, which consist of resins, additives, and photoinitiators that enable rapid curing under UV light. Together, they provide an eco-friendly alternative to traditional coatings, offering enhanced durability and finish quality for various applications.</p></p>
