<p><p>The X-Ray Crystallography Instruments market comprises two primary types: X-Ray Powder Diffraction (XRPD) and Single-Crystal X-Ray Diffraction (SCXRD). XRPD is used for analyzing crystalline materials where powdered samples provide diffraction patterns, helping to identify phases and quantify materials. In contrast, SCXRD involves single crystals, allowing for detailed three-dimensional structure determination of complex molecules, which is crucial in fields like pharmaceuticals and materials science. Both techniques are vital in advancing research and development across various industries.</p></p>
